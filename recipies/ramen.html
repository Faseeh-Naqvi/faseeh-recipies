Spicy Ramen Bowl

Description

Experience a taste of the East with this delectable Spicy Ramen Bowl. Inspired by the bustling streets of Tokyo, this dish combines chewy ramen noodles with a rich and aromatic broth. It's perfect for warming up on a rainy day or battling supernatural creatures.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, bring the broth to a simmer and add the mushrooms. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the spinach and let it wilt in the broth.
  4. Stir in the soy sauce, sesame oil, and sriracha.
  5. Divide the cooked noodles between serving bowls.
  6. Ladle the hot broth, mushrooms, and spinach over the noodles.
  7. Garnish with halved boiled eggs and sliced green onions.
